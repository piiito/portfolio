Postnatal Fitness UX Case Study

Postnatal Fitness App

UX Case Study

Empowering new mothers through tailored health and wellness solutions.

The Idea

Creating a solution that supports and empowers new mothers during the postnatal phase that is tailored for their unique challenges and focuses on postnatal exercise and health and its vital role in post partum well-being.

Problem Statement

New mothers experience feelings of fatigue, guilt, and self-disappointment because they find it challenging to juggle motherhood, maintaining a healthy lifestyle, and keeping up a social life. They don't have the time or energy to keep a exercise routine, a healthy diet, and maintain a social life. If we can solve this problem, it would impact new mothers positively by improving their physical and mental health, self-confidence and their work-life balance. It would also benefit our business because satisfied users would recommend this app to other mothers and therefore expand our reach.

My Role

  • UX Research
  • Competitive Analysis
  • Affinity Mapping
  • Prototyping

Tools Used

Google Sheets, Miro, Figma, FigJam, Adobe XD

Project duration

4 weeks

Key Insights

63% of postnatal women want to use a fitness app, yet only 12% currently do. Users seek motivation, support, and community.

Prototype & Testing

Low-fidelity wireframes and user testing helped refine the UI and improve navigation.
